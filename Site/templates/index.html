The Problem

People throw stuff away but do they really know what they're doing? No. Cal Poly's garbage system is TRASH, and we're here to help. We used coding and algorithms to figure out how to sort garbage.

A Smart Trashcan

WastEd is a trashcan that notices when you want to dispose of something, categorizes it using machine learning, and places it in the appropriate container.

Google Vision

WastEd uses the Google Vision API to determine the appropriate disposal method for each item presented.

Automatic Sorting

After WastEd determines the type of item you have, simply place it in the disposal compartment and watch it fall into the correct bin!
