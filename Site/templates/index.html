The Problem

Many students don't know where their trash belongs. Not only is this inefficient, but it is harmful to the environment. Although Cal Poly provides recycling, compost, and trash bins around campus, the majority of students pay no regard to which category is most appropriate for their waste.

A Smart Trashcan

WastEd is a smart trashcan that removes the hassle from sorting trash. It will take trash, categorize it using machine learning and image recognition, and place it into the appropriate bin.

Google Vision

WastEd uses the Google Vision API to categorize the item presented and determine its appropriate disposal method.

Automatic Sorting

After WastEd determines the type of item you have, it will flash a color corresponding to the bin it belongs in. Green for compost, blue for recycling, and red for garbage.
